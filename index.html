What is HEALS?

HEALS is a holistic framework focused on Health, Education, Agriculture/Livelihoods, Life-skills, and Sustainability — enabling self-reliance and resilience across communities.

Health & Wellbeing

Accessible primary care, awareness camps, and preventive health initiatives.

Education & Skills

Bridge learning gaps and provide employable skills for youth and women.

Livelihoods & Sustainability

Income-generating projects aligned to SDGs and disaster-risk reduction.
